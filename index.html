Hi! I'm George, a software developer in the Hartford, CT area with a background in music composition and audio editing. I'm always in search of ways to make things more usable, adaptable, and efficient. The thing that drives me is finding where beauty meets structure.

About Me

My code so far has been focused on applying the ‘rules’ and structure of music to new user interfaces, utilizing concepts like gamification to understand and manipulate music in a more accessible and physical way. I have explored areas such as stochastic systems, FFT effects, and motion capture. When I’m not coding or making music I’m tinkering and building in other ways. You can see some of my other projects here.

I am currently studying in a program called Thinkful where I am honing my skills in web-design and software development so I can help to make more user friendly and functional programs. Right now I am head over heels for JavaScript. I’m looking for ways to collaborate professionally and get involved in projects outside of my comfort zone so I can continue to grow!
